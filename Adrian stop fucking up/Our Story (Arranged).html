Our Story

Director Tan Yong Sheng founded Iot homes in 2024, with plans, and drafts for his business being made as early as 2022. Originally, he had attempted to start his business in the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur, opening a small shop, called “home-elektronic” near his family home. However, little success was credited to him during this time, as KL, was the most competitive, and pricy city of Malaysia.

With that being said, he decided to restart his business, in Petaling Jaya, where he rebranded his business as Iot home. During this time, he decided to ditch his old plan of a shop-lot business, in favour of an online selling platform. This plan was proven to be effective, as only after half-a-year since it’s opening, Iot homes already became the most-selected home-electronics brand in the West of Petaling Jaya.
